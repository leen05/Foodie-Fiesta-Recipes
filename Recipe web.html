Butter Chicken


Ingredients

  • Chicken (500g, boneless, cubed)
  • Tomato Puree (1 cup)
  • Fresh Cream (½ cup)
  • Butter (2 tbsp)
  • Kasuri Methi(1 tsp, dried fenugreek leaves)

Recipe Instructions

  1. Marinate Chicken – Mix 500g chicken with 2 tbsp yogurt, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chili powder, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Let it rest for 1-2 hours.
  2. Cook Chicken – Heat 2 tbsp butter in a pan, cook the marinated chicken until golden brown, and set aside.
  3. Prepare Gravy – In the same pan, sauté 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, then add 1 cup tomato puree, ½ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp coriander powder, and 1 tsp sugar. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Combine & Simmer – Add cooked chicken to the gravy, mix well, and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Finish & Serve – Stir in ½ cup fresh cream, 1 tsp kasuri methi, and butter. Serve hot with naan or rice! 😋🔥

Shahi Paneer


Ingredients

  • Paneer (Cottage Cheese)
  • Cashews (or Almonds)
  • Fresh Cream
  • Tomato & Onion
  • Ghee or Butter

Recipe Instructions

  1. Prepare the Base – Sauté onions, tomatoes, cashews, and ginger-garlic paste in ghee/butter, then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Cook the Gravy – Heat ghee/butter, add the blended paste, and cook with turmeric, cumin, garam masala, and salt until oil separates.
  3. Add Cream & Paneer – Pour in fresh cream, mix well, and add cubed paneer to the gravy.
  4. Simmer & Flavor – Let it cook for a few minutes, then add a pinch of sugar and kasuri methi for extra taste.
  5. Garnish & Serve – Top with chopped nuts, saffron (optional), and fresh coriander. Serve hot with naan or rice! 😋🔥

Chole Bhature


Ingredients


Chole

  • Chickpeas – 1 cup, soaked overnight & boiled.
  • Base Ingredients – 2 tomatoes (pureed), 1 onion (finely chopped), 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste.
  • Spices
  • Cooking Medium
  • Extras – Fresh coriander for garnish.

Bhature

  • Flour Mix
  • Fermenting Agents
  • Salt
  • Water –
  • Oil

Recipe Instructions

  1. Prepare Chickpeas & Dough – Soak 1 cup chickpeas overnight and pressure cook with water and salt for 3-4 whistles. Meanwhile, mix maida, semolina, salt, baking soda, and yogurt to knead a soft dough. Let it rest for 2 hours.
  2. Cook Masala – Heat oil/ghee in a pan, sauté cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste until golden. Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and amchur. Cook until oil separates.
  3. Mix & Simmer – Add boiled chickpeas to the masala, mix well, and let it simmer for 10 minutes. Adjust salt as needed.
  4. Roll & Fry Bhature – Divide the dough into equal portions, roll into oval/round shapes, and deep-fry in hot oil until puffed and golden brown.
  5. Serve & Enjoy – Garnish chole with fresh coriander and serve hot with crispy Bhature, pickles, and onions. 😋🔥

Idli Sambar


Ingredients


Idli

  • 2 cups Idli Rice
  • 1 cup Urad Dal (Split Black Gram)
  • ½ tsp Fenugreek Seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for batter)

Sambar

  • ½ cup Toor Dal (Pigeon Pea Lentils)
  • 1 Onion & 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 1 Carrot, 4-5 Drumsticks (optional, chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Sambar Powder –
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds, Curry Leaves & Hing

Recipe Instructions

  1. Prepare Idli Batter – Soak idli rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 6 hours. Grind to a smooth batter, mix, add salt, and ferment overnight.
  2. Steam Idlis – Grease idli molds, pour batter, and steam for 10-12 minutes until soft and fluffy.
  3. Cook Dal & Vegetables – Pressure cook toor dal with water for 3-4 whistles. In a pan, sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, tomatoes, and veggies until soft.
  4. Prepare Sambar – Add cooked dal, sambar powder, salt, and water. Simmer for 10 minutes until well combined.
  5. Serve & Enjoy – Serve hot, fluffy idlis with flavorful sambar and coconut chutney! 😋🔥

Spicy Red Sauce Pasta


Ingredients


Pasta

  • 2 cups Pasta (Penne/Fusilli/Spaghetti)
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 4 cups Water (for boiling)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Flakes

For Spicy Sauce

  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • ½ cup Heavy Cream or Cheese (optional)

Recipe Instructions

  1. Boil Pasta – Cook pasta in boiling salted water until al dente, then drain and keep aside.
  2. Prepare Spicy Sauce – Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic and onions until golden. Add tomato puree, tomato paste, chili powder, and cook until oil separates.
  3. Season & Simmer – Add oregano, basil, black pepper, and chili flakes. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Toss Pasta – Add boiled pasta to the sauce, mix well, and let it cook for 2 minutes to absorb flavors.
  5. Garnish & Serve – Top with cheese or fresh basil and enjoy your fiery, spicy pasta! 🌶️🔥😋
